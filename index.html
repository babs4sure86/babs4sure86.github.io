What to do when in class

  • Pay attention when lectures are being delivered and avoid distraction from others
  • Ask the lecturer questions when lessons are not clear or clarification is needed
  • Use inter-lecture free times to revise previous lessons before the next lecturer comes in

How to manage free times at home

  • Read your notes and do not wait till assessment period
  • Exercise as this helps your mental health
  • Rest when you need to and continue when you become strong again
  • Have a minimum of 8 hour sleep
